Top three activities to do in Birmingham

Attend a show or a concert

Birmingham Town Hall is a concert hall and venue for popular assemblies opened in 1834 and situated in Victoria Square, Birmingham, England.

Picnic and nature

With over 8,000 acres of open space and parks, Birmingham is one of the greenest cities in the UK, making it the perfect place to enjoy a summer picnic!

Sport and games

Birmingham is renowned across the world as a venue for competitive sport.The city has some of the most exciting venues for watching or participating in sport in the UK, including Arena Birmingham, Villa Park and Edgbaston Stadium.
